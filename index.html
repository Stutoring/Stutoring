What is Stutoring

More than tutoring.
It's a head start.

Stutoring is an academic tutoring program founded by high-achieving students who have recently navigated the exact same academic path your child is about to take. We don't just help with homework — we build the foundations that make high school feel manageable. We primarily offer individual sessions, with the option for friends or siblings to register together.

  • Designed for Grades 6–8 preparing for the jump to high school
  • Subjects: Mathematics, Science, English & French
  • Small groups ensure personalized attention every session
  • Mentors who recently excelled in Pre-AP and advanced streams
  • Builds confidence, study habits, and conceptual understanding
  • Individual sessions — or register together with friends & family

The Process

How the Program Works

A straightforward path from where your child is now — to where they need to be for high school.

1

Register & Apply

Fill out a short form. We'll match your child to the right group and subject focus.

2

Weekly Sessions

Primarily individual sessions online — or register with a friend or sibling. Focused on real understanding, not memorization.

3

Track Improvement

Parents and students receive feedback as concepts are mastered and confidence builds each week.

4

High School Ready

Your child enters Grade 9 with the foundations, habits, and confidence to succeed in advanced courses.
